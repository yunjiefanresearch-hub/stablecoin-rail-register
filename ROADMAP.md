# Roadmap

**Cadence commitment:** quarterly `diff` releases, plus ad-hoc patch releases for major
regulatory events. Each release is tagged (semver) and archived to Zenodo with a DOI.

| Version | Scope | Status |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **v0.1.0** | Schema + methodology + working build pipeline + corridor layer (1 worked example). Spine dimension (`permitted_activity_yield`) seeded for **HK** and **US §404**, verified to source. | in progress |
| v0.2.0 | Add **Taiwan** and **Brazil** across core dimensions; expand HK/US to full dimension set; **MCP server** (agent-queryable). | planned |
| v0.3.0 | Backfill **EU / UK / SG / CN** on the spine dimension (MiCA et al. as benchmark); expand corridor library. | planned |
| v0.x | Full 8-jurisdiction × 10-dimension matrix; richer `requirement_structured` facets per dimension. | planned |

Scope discipline: depth before breadth. The differentiator is rigorous, sourced, corridor-aware
coverage of a focused set, not 200 shallow jurisdictions.
