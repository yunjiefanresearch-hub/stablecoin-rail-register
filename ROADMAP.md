> **Standing liability — the unverified legal cells.** Every `compose()` "9/9 corridors, 64/66 §5.14
> categories" agreement is only as honest as the hand-curated per-jurisdiction signal table and the
> cells beneath it. **47 `tier1_legal` cells remain `unset`** — transcribed from the maintainer's
> Compliance Matrix, not yet confirmed against the official text. The `claim_class` axis, the citable
> gates, and the signal-provenance discipline are the right *defenses* and they bound the blast radius,
> but **no single feature retires this liability — only the verification pass does.** It is the reason
> the verification pass is bundled into v0.9.0 rather than deferred indefinitely, and it is tracked in
> the verification queue (`COVERAGE.md`, MCP `verification_report()`).

Scope discipline: **depth before breadth.** The differentiator is rigorous, sourced,
corridor-aware coverage of a focused set — not 200 shallow jurisdictions. Planned cells are
visible in `COVERAGE.md` (⬜vX.Y) on purpose: this is an actively-built standard, not a finished table.
